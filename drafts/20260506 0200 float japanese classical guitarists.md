japanese classical guitar artists

Japan has a rich history of classical guitar, producing some of the world's most technically proficient performers and renowned luthiers. The scene is characterized by a blend of rigorous technical mastery and an "orchestral" approach to the instrument. \[[1](https://www.guitarsalon.com/blog/recording-artist-kazuhito-yamashita?srsltid=AfmBOoq8Li4N06alUQHAyAj9e4uxtaS7POdo3bmVB6lXfFbymyyUGnl6), [2](https://www.facebook.com/musiciangoods/videos/kazuhito-yamashita-became-one-of-the-most-technically-astonishing-classical-guit/2350366415368842/), [3](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ichiko%5FAoba)]

Prominent Classical Guitarists

**Kazuhito Yamashita (1961–2026)**: Widely considered one of the greatest classical guitarists in history, Yamashita was known for his breathtaking speed, precision, and [unprecedented transcriptions](https://www.facebook.com/musiciangoods/videos/kazuhito-yamashita-became-one-of-the-most-technically-astonishing-classical-guit/2350366415368842/) of full orchestral works like Mussorgsky’s _Pictures at an Exhibition_.

***

**Kaori Muraji**: A celebrated figure in Japan, she is known for her expressive performances and numerous recordings on major labels like Decca.

***

**Shin-Ichi Fukuda**: A leading performer and teacher who has won prestigious awards including the Paris International Guitar Competition.

***

**Kanahi Yamashita**: A rising star and daughter of Kazuhito Yamashita, she continues the family legacy with a focus on both [traditional and contemporary repertoire](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PNmekXjwp2Q).

***

**Nanae Fujiwara**: A performer celebrated for her technique and expressive interpretations of [Spanish classical repertoire](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1R6%5FbAcAJgc). \[[1](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Category:Japanese%5Fclassical%5Fguitarists), [2](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=apPg7A8E7uA), [3](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PNmekXjwp2Q#:~:text=We%20visited%20Kanahi%20Yamahita%20in%20Nagoya%2C%20Japan,of%20our%20guitars.%200:00%20Introduction%200:16%20%2A'Inventions%2C), [4](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1R6%5FbAcAJgc#:~:text=Ms.%20Nanae%20Fijiwara%20is%20a%20spectacular%20performer,and%20expressive%20performance%20of%20the%20Spanish%20Classical), [5](https://www.guitarsalon.com/blog/recording-artist-kazuhito-yamashita?srsltid=AfmBOoq8Li4N06alUQHAyAj9e4uxtaS7POdo3bmVB6lXfFbymyyUGnl6), [6](https://www.facebook.com/musiciangoods/videos/kazuhito-yamashita-became-one-of-the-most-technically-astonishing-classical-guit/2350366415368842/)]

Historic Pioneers

- **Morishige Takei (1890–1949)**: Often called the "elder statesman" of Japanese guitar music, he penned some of the [earliest known Japanese compositions](https://classical-scene.com/2018/11/16/yamashita-guitar/) for the instrument.
- **Yoshie Okawara (1903–1935)**: A pioneer who recorded some of the first guitar music in Japan and composed suites celebrating Japanese nature, such as _A Bouquet_. \[[1](https://classical-scene.com/2018/11/16/yamashita-guitar/), [2](https://www.digitalguitararchive.com/2012/02/the-early-guitar-and-important-guitarists-in-japan/)]

Renowned Luthiers (Guitar Makers) \[[1](https://www.namm.org/library/oral-history/kazuo-yairi)]

The quality of Japanese-made classical guitars is world-renowned, with several masters setting global standards: \[[1](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ichiko%5FAoba), [2](https://jobsinjapan.com/living-in-japan-guide/the-joy-of-being-a-guitarist-in-japan/)]

- **Masaru Kohno (1926–1998)**: Perhaps the [most important Japanese luthier](https://www.guitarsalon.com/luthier/sakurai-kohno), his instruments have been used by legends like Julian Bream and Sharon Isbin.
- **Masaki Sakurai**: Kohno's nephew, who won the [Paris Guitar Making Competition](https://www.kohno-guitar.org/en/index.html) in 1988 and continues the Kohno workshop's legacy.
- **Eich Kodaira**: A master luthier known for founding the Asturias guitar label and producing high-grade concert instruments under his own name. \[[1](https://www.kohno-guitar.org/en/index.html), [2](https://www.guitarsalon.com/luthier/sakurai-kohno?srsltid=AfmBOorpPg1KxXP0-GaQ%5FykpNlspviEYJlDCt%5FsjSMcrsDWn5yp1Cm29), [3](https://www.google.com/search?q=product\&prds=pvt%3Ahg%2Cproductid%3A8858706722314479665\&ibp=oshop\&gl=us), [4](https://www.google.com/search?q=product\&prds=pvt%3Ahg%2Cproductid%3A13305128365312458518\&ibp=oshop\&gl=us), [5](https://www.google.com/search?q=product\&prds=pvt%3Ahg%2Cproductid%3A16619482653817469721\&ibp=oshop\&gl=us)]
