variant or variation

Use <mark style="margin: 0px; text-decoration-style: solid; text-decoration-color: rgb(230, 232, 240); border-bottom: 0px none rgb(230, 232, 240);"><strong>variant</strong> to describe a specific, distinct version of a thing, and <strong>variation</strong> to describe the act of changing or a general shift in levels, amounts, or presentation</mark>. \[[1](https://dictionary.cambridge.org/dictionary/english/variation), [2](https://www.facebook.com/groups/englishquestionsanswered/posts/2466573393629337/)]

While they look similar and occasionally overlap, they serve distinct roles in English. \[[1](https://ell.stackexchange.com/questions/192990/what-are-the-differences-between-variation-and-variant), [2](https://www.homophone.com/h/variance-variants)]

### The Core Difference

- **Variant (Noun/Adjective)**: A **specific object or distinct version** that differs from a standard or from others in its class. It is a tangible, concrete entity.
- **Variation (Noun)**: The **process, act, or measurement of change**, or a slight difference in condition, amount, or level. It often refers to fluctuations or the concept of diversity. \[[1](https://english.stackexchange.com/questions/148800/what-is-the-difference-between-a-variant-and-a-variation), [2](https://www.ldoceonline.com/dictionary/variant), [3](https://www.decidr.ai/university/glossary/based-on-the-variant-CZ6nZRCB1dugGR), [4](https://www.quora.com/Should-I-use-Product-variant-or-Product-variation), [5](https://www.collinsdictionary.com/us/dictionary/english/variation), [6](https://ell.stackexchange.com/questions/192990/what-are-the-differences-between-variation-and-variant)]

### Everyday Examples

- **Product Choice**: A shoe model comes in blue, red, and black. Each color is a **product variant**. The factory line has a slight **variation in color** due to dye lots.
- **Science & Healthcare**: Scientists identified a highly contagious **virus variant**. A patient might experience a **variation in heart rate** throughout the day.
- **Language & Art**: "Color" and "colour" are **spelling variants** of the same word. The musician played five creative **variations on a theme**. \[[1](https://www.quora.com/Should-I-use-Product-variant-or-Product-variation), [2](https://www.ptc.com/en/blogs/alm/variant-vs-version?srsltid=AfmBOorDbSKwFx5lcl7xIqQ6KL3sliOJQu3h4cYSR8UmNfxDA%5FW3XljH), [3](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/variant), [4](https://ell.stackexchange.com/questions/192990/what-are-the-differences-between-variation-and-variant), [5](https://dictionary.cambridge.org/dictionary/english/variation), [6](https://www.ldoceonline.com/dictionary/variant), [7](https://www.dictionary.com/browse/variant), [8](https://www.collinsdictionary.com/us/dictionary/english/variation)]

### How to Choose

- Choose **variant** if you can point to a specific, separate item, option, or option category (e.g., _"Download the mobile variant"_ or _"Select a size variant"_).
- Choose **variation** if you are describing a shift, trend, deviation, or broad diversity over time or space (e.g., _"regional variations in accent"_ or _"variation in seasonal temperatures"_). \[[1](https://www.quora.com/Should-I-use-Product-variant-or-Product-variation), [2](https://www.decidr.ai/university/glossary/based-on-the-variant-CZ6nZRCB1dugGR), [3](https://www.facebook.com/groups/englishquestionsanswered/posts/2466573393629337/), [4](https://www.collinsdictionary.com/us/dictionary/english/variation), [5](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/dynamics365/business-central/inventory-item-variants)]
