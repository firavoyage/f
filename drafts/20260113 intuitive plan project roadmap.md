  - Which customer problems matter most

  - Large chunks of work that support a theme
  - Often cross teams
  - Still about outcomes, not tasks

- **6. Features**

  - Specific solutions users will see
  - Designed, debated, adjusted
  - Can change without breaking the roadmap

- **7. Stories / Tasks**

  - Small, actionable work items
  - Owned by teams and individuals
  - Highly flexible

- **Leadership** lives mostly at levels 1‚Äì4
- **Product managers** live at levels 3‚Äì6
