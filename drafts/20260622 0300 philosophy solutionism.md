solutionism

**Solutionism** is <mark style="font-family: &quot;Google Sans&quot;, Roboto, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; font-weight: 500; margin: 0px; text-decoration: none solid rgb(230, 232, 240); border-bottom: 0px none rgb(230, 232, 240);">the belief or ideology that all complex social, political, and human problems have clean, definitive solutions, typically achieved through technology, data, and efficiency</mark>. \[[1](https://dictionary.cambridge.org/us/dictionary/english/solutionism), [2](https://everydayconcepts.io/solutionism)]

The term was popularized by technology critic Evgeny Morozov in his 2013 book, _[To Save Everything, Click Here: The Folly of Technological Solutionism](https://www.newamerica.org/insights/to-save-everything-click-here/)_. Morozov argues that solutionism is deeply embedded in Silicon Valley culture, creating an mindset where apps, algorithms, and automated tracking are treated as universal fixes for deeply rooted systemic issues. \[[1](https://www.theguardian.com/technology/2013/mar/09/evgeny-morozov-technology-solutionism-interview), [2](https://www.newamerica.org/insights/to-save-everything-click-here/), [3](https://www.genevapolicyoutlook.ch/beyond-techno-solutionism-and-silver-bullets/), [4](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uhU0hRng-eE\&t=33)]

### Core Characteristics of Solutionism

- **Solutions in Search of Problems:** Working backward by developing an advanced tool or AI model first, and then scanning the world for a problem to apply it to.
- **Reductionism:** Simplifying messy, subjective, and nuanced human realities (like education, crime, or politics) into transactional data puzzles that can be "optimized".
- **Shifting Responsibility:** Recasting collective, institutional flaws as individual failures. For instance, tracking consumer carbon footprints rather than regulating polluting industries, or tracking personal fitness data instead of addressing systemic healthcare access.
- **Ignoring the Root Causes:** Over-focusing on immediate, superficial "bugs" or symptoms rather than addressing the structural, historical, or socio-economic origins of an issue. \[[1](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VgDlVboDAtQ\&vl=en), [2](https://timwoodpowell.com/solutionism-whose-problems-does-it-solve/), [3](https://www.tandfonline.com/doi/full/10.1080/09687599.2025.2548461), [4](https://stimpunks.org/glossary/techno-solutionism/), [5](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uhU0hRng-eE\&t=33)]

### Key Examples

- **Smart Cities:** Deploying automated sensors to optimize traffic or pedestrian paths while neglecting larger, more impactful investments in public transit infrastructure and long-term urban planning.
- **AI and Predictive Policing:** Using predictive algorithms to combat crime. While intended to optimize resources, these tools often rely on flawed, biased datasets, which reinforces over-policing in specific communities rather than tackling the root socioeconomic drivers of crime.
- **EdTech Saturation:** Treating the complex art of education as a simple matter of transmitting online content and evaluating students through automated quizzes, rather than addressing student poverty or teaching conditions. \[[1](https://www.tandfonline.com/doi/full/10.1080/09687599.2025.2548461), [2](https://epale.ec.europa.eu/en/blog/technological-solutionism), [3](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VgDlVboDAtQ\&vl=en), [4](https://ssir.org/articles/entry/disrupting%5Fthe%5Fgospel%5Fof%5Ftech%5Fsolutionism%5Fto%5Fbuild%5Ftech%5Fjustice), [5](https://www.unesco.org/en/articles/avoiding-solutionism-digital-transformation-education)]

### Why It Is Criticized

Critics warn that solutionism creates a false sense of progress while introducing new "technological debt" and unforeseen moral hazards. When complex civic duties and political negotiations are gamified or automated, the intrinsic motivations for ethical behavior can be permanently corrupted. Ultimately, some human struggles cannot be cleanly fixed; they must be collaboratively managed through politics, policy, and compromise. \[[1](https://www.reddit.com/r/developpeurs/comments/1lkvjbx/comment%5Fluter%5Fcontre%5Fle%5Fsolutionnisme/?tl=en), [2](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s11948-024-00524-x), [3](https://www.newamerica.org/insights/to-save-everything-click-here/), [4](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9yQqrZUD6Gk\&t=39), [5](https://dictionary.cambridge.org/us/dictionary/english/solutionism), [6](https://everydayconcepts.io/solutionism)]
