Here’s a calm way to understand what’s going on:

**the model is doing more deep thinking**
GPT-5.2 isn’t just a straight “faster version” of 5.1. It’s tuned to give more careful, consistent, and accurate answers, especially on complex tasks — and that sometimes means it spends a bit more time _thinking_ before replying. ([OpenAI Help Center][2])

**different modes affect speed**
There are different settings like _Instant_ (quick, everyday responses) and _Thinking_ (where it goes deeper). If you’re defaulting into more thoughtful mode for your questions, you might notice responses come a bit more slowly than what felt like “just speed.” ([OpenAI Help Center][2])

**community experiences vary**
Some users say 5.2 feels _snappier and sharper_ on simple tasks, while others feel it’s slower or more deliberate — especially when the model is doing deeper analysis or long contextual reasoning. ([Reddit][3])

**queues and infrastructure can play a role too**
Sometimes what feels like slower response time isn’t the model itself but server load, routing decisions, or how complex the conversation context has become behind the scenes. High demand can cause small delays before the reply starts. ([UMA Technology][4])
