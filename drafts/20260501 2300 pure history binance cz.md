Changpeng Zhao's background is characterized by a transition from a modest immigrant upbringing to a career in high-frequency trading and eventual leadership in the cryptocurrency industry. \[[1](https://www.binance.com/en/square/post/23520489391497), [2](https://www.trainy.co/en/blog/Changpeng-Zhao-biography)]

Early Life and Immigration

- **Birth in China**: Born in 1977 in Jiangsu, China, into a family of academics. His father, a university professor, was labeled a "pro-bourgeois intellectual" and temporarily exiled shortly after CZ's birth.
- **Move to Canada**: At age 12, following the 1989 Tiananmen Square protests, he and his family immigrated to Vancouver, Canada.
- **Work Ethic**: As a teenager, he held several service-sector jobs to support his family, including flipping burgers at McDonald’s and working night shifts at a gas station. \[[1](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Changpeng%5FZhao), [2](https://macleans.ca/economy/business/who-is-changpeng-zhao-canadas-crypto-king/), [3](https://www.forbes.com/profile/changpeng-zhao/), [4](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Changpeng%5FZhao), [5](https://www.shortform.com/podcast/episode/all-in-with-chamath-jason-sacks-friedberg-2026-02-10-episode-summary-cz-s-untold-story-the-rise-fall-and-redemption-of-binance-s-founder)]

Education and Early Career

- **Academic Background**: He studied computer science at **McGill University** in Montreal, where he developed a deep interest in financial trading systems.
- **Tokyo Stock Exchange**: After graduating, he worked in Tokyo as a developer for the exchange’s order-matching system.
- **Bloomberg Tradebook**: He later spent four years at Bloomberg in New York, where he was promoted three times and managed a global team of 80 people developing futures trading software.
- **First Startup**: In 2005, he moved to Shanghai to launch **Fusion Systems**, which specialized in high-frequency trading platforms for stockbrokers. \[[1](https://www.binance.com/en/square/post/13757987141873), [2](https://www.binance.com/en/square/post/14048989945410), [3](https://www.binance.com/en/square/post/13757987141873), [4](https://www.binance.com/en/square/post/310479593845858), [5](https://kanga.exchange/university/en/courses/beginner-course/lessons/72-who-is-changpeng-zhao-ceo-of-binance/), [6](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Changpeng%5FZhao), [7](https://www.shortform.com/podcast/episode/all-in-with-chamath-jason-sacks-friedberg-2026-02-10-episode-summary-cz-s-untold-story-the-rise-fall-and-redemption-of-binance-s-founder)]

Transition to Cryptocurrency

- **Introduction to Bitcoin**: In 2013, he learned about Bitcoin during a poker game with Bobby Lee (founder of BTCC).
- **All-In Approach**: Convinced of its potential, he sold his Shanghai apartment in 2014 to invest everything into Bitcoin.
- **Pre-Binance Roles**: Before founding Binance in 2017, he served as the Head of Development at Blockchain.info and later as the Chief Technology Officer (CTO) of the exchange OKCoin. \[[1](https://www.binance.com/en/square/post/22296548518457), [2](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Changpeng%5FZhao), [3](https://www.binance.com/en/square/post/14048989945410), [4](https://www.binance.com/en/square/post/310479593845858)]

family background and childhood

Changpeng Zhao's childhood was defined by academic roots, political exile in China, and a frugal, hardworking immigrant experience in Canada. Born in 1977 in Jiangsu province, his family faced severe hardship during the Cultural Revolution. \[[1](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Changpeng%5FZhao), [2](https://trustwallet.com/blog/web3/who-is-cz-changpeng-zhao), [3](https://fortune.com/2023/04/14/binance-changpeng-zhao/)]

Family Background in China

- **Academic Heritage**: Both of his parents were schoolteachers. His father, **Shengkai Zhao**, was a university professor of geophysics and a "math whiz".
- **Political Persecution**: Shortly after Zhao’s birth, his father was branded a "pro-bourgeois intellectual" and exiled to a rural area.
- **Living Conditions**: In Hefei, the family of four lived in a single faculty dormitory room with shared bunk beds and a communal kitchen in the hallway. They later moved to a tiny two-bedroom apartment where the master bedroom doubled as a living room. \[[1](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Changpeng%5FZhao), [2](https://www.binance.com/en/square/post/21740433438321), [3](https://macleans.ca/economy/business/who-is-changpeng-zhao-canadas-crypto-king/), [4](https://www.binance.com/en/square/post/310206408285761), [5](https://www.weex.com/news/detail/zhao-changpengs-autobiography-stories-about-family-rivals-and-friends-632243)]

Immigration and Childhood in Canada

- **Flight to Vancouver**: In late 1989, at age 12, Zhao and his family emigrated to Canada following the Tiananmen Square protests to join his father, who was pursuing a doctorate at the [University of British Columbia (UBC)](https://www.ubc.ca/).
- **Modest Living**: The family initially lived in a UBC student dormitory. Zhao reportedly first tasted fresh milk—a luxury in China at the time—only after arriving in Vancouver.
- **Teenage Jobs**: To help cover household expenses, Zhao worked several service-sector jobs, including flipping burgers at **McDonald's** and working overnight shifts at a **Chevron gas station**.
- **Tech Influence**: Despite their tight budget, his father spent $7,000 CAD—roughly seven months' income—on an x286 computer for Zhao. This early exposure led him to begin programming in high school. \[[1](https://macleans.ca/economy/business/who-is-changpeng-zhao-canadas-crypto-king/), [2](https://trustwallet.com/blog/web3/who-is-cz-changpeng-zhao), [3](https://www.weex.com/news/detail/zhao-changpengs-autobiography-stories-about-family-rivals-and-friends-632243), [4](https://www.forbes.com/profile/changpeng-zhao/)]

Siblings

- **Older Sister**: Zhao has an older sister who was instrumental in his early life. She began working at McDonald's at age 15 and helped him get his first job there a year later. In Vancouver, she famously traded rooms with him periodically so he could sleep in a room with a window. \[[1](https://www.binance.com/en/square/post/310206408285761), [2](https://www.binance.com/en-IN/square/post/7230848859889)]
