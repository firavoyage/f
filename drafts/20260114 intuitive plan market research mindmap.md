What could be the purpose

---

The purpose of market research for app development, Fira, is really about **understanding before building**. Big companies do it so they don’t guess—they make decisions based on real insight. Here’s how I’d gently break it down:

---

**1. Understand the Users**

- Who they are, what they need, and what problems frustrate them.
- Learn their habits and what they value in an app.

**2. Reduce Risk**

- Avoid spending time and money on features nobody wants.
- See if there’s a real market for your idea before launching.

**3. Spot Opportunities**

- Discover gaps in the market that competitors haven’t solved.
- Find ways to make your app unique and valuable.

**4. Inform Design and Features**

- Decide which features matter most.
- Create a user experience that feels natural and helpful.

**5. Shape Marketing Strategy**

- Understand how to reach and communicate with your audience.
- Know what messaging will resonate and attract users.

**6. Guide Growth**

- Identify trends and behaviors that can help your app evolve.
- Learn from feedback to improve and scale sustainably.

---

In short, market research is like **listening to the world before you speak with your app**. It’s gentle, careful preparation that saves you from costly mistakes and helps your idea truly connect.
