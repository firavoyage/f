can i remove the password on phantom

No, you cannot completely remove the password (or PIN) requirement from your Phantom wallet, as it is a fundamental security feature used to encrypt and unlock your wallet locally. \[[1](https://help.phantom.com/hc/en-us/articles/44387539444883-Change-password-in-the-Phantom-browser-extension), [2](https://help.phantom.com/hc/en-us/sections/28126813836435-Manage-accounts-and-settings), [3](https://help.phantom.com/hc/en-us/articles/4406393769875-Reset-password-in-the-Phantom-browser-extension)]

Wallet Security Details

- **Browser Extension**: The password is required to unlock your wallet every time the browser is restarted or the extension locks. While you cannot remove it, you can change it at any time in **Settings** > **Security & Privacy** > **Change Password**.
- **Mobile App**: The app uses a 4-digit PIN or biometric security (Face ID/Fingerprint). You can manage these in **Settings** > **Security & Privacy** > **Auto-Lock**, where you can adjust the timer, though you cannot disable the lock entirely. \[[1](https://help.phantom.com/hc/en-us/articles/50198015050003-Why-doesn-t-Phantom-have-a-log-out-button), [2](https://help.phantom.com/hc/en-us/articles/44387539444883-Change-password-in-the-Phantom-browser-extension), [3](https://help.phantom.com/hc/en-us/articles/4406393769875-Reset-password-in-the-Phantom-browser-extension)]

If You Forget Your Password

If you lose your password, **Phantom cannot recover it for you** because they do not store your credentials. Your only option is to reset the wallet: \[[1](https://help.phantom.com/hc/en-us/articles/4406393755411-What-if-I-lost-my-recovery-phrase-private-key-or-PIN)]

1. Click **Forgot password** → **Reset & wipe app** (this will delete the current local instance of your wallet).
2. Select **I already have a wallet**.
3. Restore your funds using your **Secret Recovery Phrase**. \[[1](https://help.phantom.com/hc/en-us/articles/4406393769875-Reset-password-in-the-Phantom-browser-extension), [2](https://help.phantom.com/hc/en-us/articles/18827673580563-Reset-the-Phantom-app), [3](https://help.phantom.com/hc/en-us/articles/49409366802067-How-your-funds-are-stored)]

**Warning:** If you do not have your 12-word Secret Recovery Phrase, you will lose access to your funds permanently after a reset. \[[1](https://help.phantom.com/hc/en-us/articles/49409366802067-How-your-funds-are-stored), [2](https://help.phantom.com/hc/en-us/articles/4406393755411-What-if-I-lost-my-recovery-phrase-private-key-or-PIN), [3](https://www.coinjar.com/uk/learn/how-to-set-up-phantom-wallet)]

Do you have your **Secret Recovery Phrase** backed up in a safe, offline location? \[[1](https://help.phantom.com/hc/en-us/articles/36482144712083-Get-started-with-Phantom)]

For the Quickest Experience

- **Extend the Auto-Lock Timer**: If you are using the browser extension, you can set the wallet to stay unlocked for up to **one full day** so you don't have to keep re-entering the password.
  1. Click your **profile avatar** (top left).
  2. Go to **Settings** > **Security & Privacy**.
  3. Click **Auto-Lock Timer** and select a longer duration (e.g., 8 hours or 1 day).
- **Enable Biometrics (Mobile Only)**: If you are on the mobile app, you can use **Face ID or Fingerprint** instead of typing a PIN, which is nearly instantaneous.
- **Turn on Auto-Confirm**: For specific apps you trust, you can enable "Auto-Confirm" in **Settings** > **Connected Apps**. can i enable google oauth on phantom wallet

Yes, you can enable Google OAuth on Phantom Wallet, but only if you use it to **create a new wallet** or when using apps that integrate **Phantom Connect**. \[[1](https://phantom.com/learn/guides/how-to-create-a-new-wallet), [2](https://phantom.com/connect), [3](https://docs.phantom.com/sdks/react-sdk)]

If you already have a wallet secured by a 12-word recovery phrase, you cannot retroactively "link" it to Google OAuth for login. Instead, Google login creates a separate, "seedless" wallet. \[[1](https://help.phantom.com/hc/en-us/articles/13515761228051-Security-tips-for-Phantom-users), [2](https://help.phantom.com/hc/en-us/articles/41898802028179-How-wallets-sync-across-devices-in-Phantom), [3](https://www.coinjar.com/uk/learn/how-to-set-up-phantom-wallet), [4](https://phantom.com/learn/guides/how-to-create-a-new-wallet)]

How to Use Google OAuth with Phantom

Phantom supports Google OAuth through its "Continue with Email" feature, which uses self-custodial key management to ensure Phantom never has access to your private keys. \[[1](https://phantom.com/learn/guides/how-to-create-a-new-wallet), [2](https://help.phantom.com/hc/en-us/articles/13515761228051-Security-tips-for-Phantom-users)]

- **Creating a New Wallet:**
  - **Browser Extension:** Open the extension, select **Create a New Wallet**, then click **Continue with Email** and choose **Google**.
  - **Mobile App:** Tap **Create a New Wallet**, select **Continue with Email**, and then tap **Continue with Google**.
- **Authentication & Recovery:**
  - When using Google OAuth, you must also set a **four-digit PIN**.
  - This email and PIN combination is required to restore your wallet on a new device.
- **For Developers:**
  - You can integrate Google OAuth into your own apps using the **[Phantom Connect SDK](https://docs.phantom.com/wallet-sdks-overview)**, which allows users to sign in instantly without needing to install the Phantom extension. \[[1](https://docs.phantom.com/sdks/browser-sdk), [2](https://help.phantom.com/hc/en-us/articles/32775281256851-Create-a-new-Phantom-wallet-with-Google-or-Apple), [3](https://docs.phantom.com/sdks/react-sdk), [4](https://phantom.com/learn/guides/how-to-create-a-new-wallet), [5](https://help.phantom.com/hc/en-us/articles/41898802028179-How-wallets-sync-across-devices-in-Phantom), [6](https://phantom.com/connect)]

Important Security Considerations

- **Non-Transferable:** You cannot add Google OAuth as a login layer to an existing recovery phrase-based wallet.
- **Backup Recommendation:** Even though it is "seedless," Phantom still recommends exporting your **12-word Secret Recovery Phrase** from the settings as an additional backup once the wallet is created.
- **Privacy:** While convenient, using Google OAuth can link your identity to your wallet address if you move funds between it and centralized exchanges. \[[1](https://coinledger.io/agencies/does-phantom-report-to-the-irs), [2](https://phantom.com/learn/guides/how-to-create-a-new-wallet), [3](https://help.phantom.com/hc/en-us/articles/41898802028179-How-wallets-sync-across-devices-in-Phantom), [4](https://help.phantom.com/hc/en-us/articles/13515761228051-Security-tips-for-Phantom-users)]
