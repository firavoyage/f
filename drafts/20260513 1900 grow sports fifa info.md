2026 world cup info

The **2026 FIFA World Cup** will take place from <mark style="margin: 0px; text-decoration-style: solid; text-decoration-color: rgb(230, 232, 240); border-bottom: 0px none rgb(230, 232, 240);"><strong>June 11 to July 19, 2026</strong></mark>, jointly hosted by the **United States, Mexico, and Canada**. \[[1](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/2026%5FFIFA%5FWorld%5FCup)]

Key Tournament Dates

- **Opening Match:** June 11, 2026, featuring co-host Mexico playing South Africa at the iconic [Mexico City Stadium (Estadio Azteca)](https://www.fifa.com/en/tournaments/mens/worldcup/canadamexicousa2026/articles/fifa-world-cup-2026-hosts-cities-dates-usa-mexico-canada).
- **USMNT Opening Match:** June 12, 2026, against Paraguay at SoFi Stadium in Los Angeles.
- **Canada Opening Match:** June 12, 2026, against Bosnia and Herzegovina at BMO Field in Toronto.
- **The Final:** July 19, 2026, hosted at the [New York New Jersey Stadium (MetLife Stadium)](https://www.fifa.com/en/tournaments/mens/worldcup/canadamexicousa2026/articles/new-york-new-jersey-stadium-host-world-cup-2026-final). \[[1](https://africa.espn.com/football/story/%5F/id/48677225/fifa-world-cup-power-rankings-spain-brazil-germany-england-argentina), [2](https://www.fifa.com/en/tournaments/mens/worldcup/canadamexicousa2026/articles/fifa-world-cup-2026-hosts-cities-dates-usa-mexico-canada), [3](https://losangelesfwc26.com/), [4](https://support.google.com/knowledgepanel/answer/9787176?gl=us), [5](https://www.fifa.com/en/tournaments/mens/worldcup/canadamexicousa2026/articles/match-schedule-fixtures-results-teams-stadiums), [6](https://www.fifa.com/en/tournaments/mens/worldcup/canadamexicousa2026/articles/new-york-new-jersey-stadium-host-world-cup-2026-final)]

Expanded Format

- **Teams:** 48 national teams (up from 32).
- **Matches:** A record-breaking 104 matches played over 39 days.
- **Group Stage:** 12 groups of four teams.
- **Knockout Stage:** The top two teams from each group, plus the eight best third-place finishers, advance to a new **Round of 32** knockout phase.
- **The Path to Victory:** Teams must now play eight matches total to win the trophy instead of seven. \[[1](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/D8BFwszzi1g), [2](https://www.fifa.com/en/tournaments/mens/worldcup/canadamexicousa2026/articles/opening-ceremony-usa)]
