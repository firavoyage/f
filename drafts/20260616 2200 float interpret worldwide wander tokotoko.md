## ワールドワイドワンダー TOKOTOKO meaning

The title **"ワールドワイドワンダー"** translates to <mark style="margin: 0px; text-decoration-style: solid; text-decoration-color: rgb(230, 232, 240); border-bottom: 0px none rgb(230, 232, 240);"><strong>"Worldwide Wander"</strong></mark> (or "Worldwide Wonder" as a wordplay) and is a popular Vocaloid song created by the producer **TOKOTOKO (also known as Nishizawasan-P)**. \[[1](https://vocaloid.fandom.com/wiki/%E3%83%AF%E3%83%BC%E3%83%AB%E3%83%89%E3%83%AF%E3%82%A4%E3%83%89%E3%83%AF%E3%83%B3%E3%83%80%E3%83%BC%5F%28Worldwide%5FWander%29), [2](https://projectsekai.fandom.com/wiki/Worldwide%5FWander)]

Here is a breakdown of the meaning behind the title, the artist, and the context of the song:

### Title Meaning

- **ワールドワイド (Worldwide):** Extending or reaching throughout the entire world.
- **ワンダー (Wander / Wonder):** The official English title uses **"Wander"** (to walk or roam around aimlessly), which fits the energetic, journey-like theme of the song. However, it is also a clever Japanese pun on **"Wonder"** (amazement or marvel), reflecting a sense of global excitement. \[[1](https://vocaloid.fandom.com/wiki/%E3%83%AF%E3%83%BC%E3%83%AB%E3%83%89%E3%83%AF%E3%82%A4%E3%83%89%E3%83%AF%E3%83%B3%E3%83%80%E3%83%BC%5F%28Worldwide%5FWander%29), [2](https://www.ei-navi.jp/dictionary/content/worldwide/)]

### Artist Context

- **TOKOTOKO (西沢さんP):** A famous Vocaloid producer known for his upbeat, guitar-driven pop-rock tracks.
- **The Song:** Released in December 2021, the track originally features Hatsune Miku.
- **Project SEKAI:** It was specifically commissioned as a character focus song for Hanasato Minori from the idol group **MORE MORE JUMP!** in the mobile game [_Project SEKAI COLORFUL STAGE!_](https://projectsekai.fandom.com/wiki/Worldwide%5FWander). The lyrics embody Minori's tireless, optimistic journey to spread cheer and become a global idol. \[[1](https://silentblue.remywiki.com/Worldwide%5Fwander), [2](https://vocaloid.fandom.com/wiki/%E3%83%AF%E3%83%BC%E3%83%AB%E3%83%89%E3%83%AF%E3%82%A4%E3%83%89%E3%83%AF%E3%83%B3%E3%83%80%E3%83%BC%5F%28Worldwide%5FWander%29), [3](https://projectsekai.fandom.com/wiki/Worldwide%5FWander)]

Here is a deep dive into the meaning and story behind the lyrics of **"Worldwide Wander"**.

As a song written for **Minori Hanasato** (from _Project SEKAI's_ idol group MORE MORE JUMP!), the lyrics reflect her specific journey: an ordinary girl who failed dozens of auditions but never gave up on her dream to become an idol who delivers hope to others.

***

### 1. Overcoming the Overwhelm (Verse 1)

> _“Capacity over, running around in circles every day / Head tangled up, warning warning”_\
> _“Do you need a passport for space travel?”_

**The Meaning:** The opening captures the chaotic, overwhelming reality of trying to achieve a massive dream. Minori's life is a whirlwind of training and failure. Her mind wanders to childlike, whimsical thoughts (rabbits on the moon, space travel). This shows her daydreaming, imaginative nature—she is someone who looks at the vast universe and wonders "how do I get there?" rather than giving up because it seems too far.

### 2. Co-Creating the Future (Pre-Chorus)

> _“What color will the tomorrow of the world we walk in be? / A color for the two of us, painted by you and me.”_\
> _“The world we live in is one color per person / That’s the color of love from mom and dad.”_

**The Meaning:** This introduces the core idol-fan relationship. The future isn't fixed; it is a blank canvas. Minori isn't just performing _at_ the listener—she is painting a "two-person color" _with_ them. The second pre-chorus acknowledges that everyone is born unique because of the love they received, and she wants to celebrate those individual "colors."

### 3. "Checkmate" & The Relentless Idol (Chorus)

> _“I shall checkmate, checkmate you / When I want to see you, I’ll go see you”_\
> _“From the back of the earth to inside your dreams / Fluttering, a skirt sways”_\
> _“From the back of the desk to the 22nd century”_

**The Meaning:** "Checkmate" is a bold, playful declaration. In chess, checkmate means there is no escape. Here, it means **"I am absolutely going to capture your heart and make you smile."**\
The song highlights the "Wander" theme by breaking all boundaries to reach the fan. Distance (the other side of the earth), consciousness (inside dreams), and time (the 22nd century, a nod to Doraemon's desk drawer) cannot stop her.

### 4. Breaking the Rules of Fear (Chorus continued)

> _“Escort, escorting you / Doing things by the manual is boring, right?”_\
> _“The amount of hope equals the number of anxieties / It’s supposed to be sparkling.”_

**The Meaning:** Minori didn't take the traditional, perfect path to become an idol; she failed repeatedly. "By the manual" doesn't work for her. Instead, she flips anxiety on its head. In her philosophy, **feeling incredibly anxious just means you have an equal amount of hope** trying to break through. The line _"It's supposed to be sparkling"_ is her mantra—reminding herself that life, despite the mess, is meant to shine.

### 5. Rejecting Passivity (Verse 2)

> _“Is it true that you should just sleep until good luck arrives? / No way, there's no way I can just wait!”_\
> _“In perfect condition, always in perfect condition / A one-way ticket is fine, right?”_

**The Meaning:** This targets the Japanese idiom _“Kafuu wa nete mate”_ (Good luck comes to those who wait). Minori aggressively rejects this. She cannot sit still. Buying a "one-way ticket" means she has committed 100% to this idol journey with **no backup plan and no regrets**.

### 6. Healing the Past through the Present (Bridge)

> _“My chest tightens / So that we can laugh at the unerasable past...”_\
> _“If a song comes on that you can dance to, dancing to it is the secret to enjoying yourself!”_

**The Meaning:** Minori has a painful history of rejection, and her fans likely have their own regrets or "unerasable pasts." The bridge serves as a emotional release. You can't erase the bad days, but you can dance through the present so that, eventually, those painful memories just become part of a story you can laugh about later.

***

### Summary of the Message

"Worldwide Wander" is an anthem for **reckless, joyful effort**. It argues that being messy, anxious, and unconventional is completely fine, as long as you keep moving forward. It redefines the idol's job: not to be a perfect, untouchable figure, but to be a clumsy, fiercely loyal companion who will travel through time and space just to make sure you are smiling.
