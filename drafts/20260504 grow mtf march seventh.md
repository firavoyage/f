In the context of the video game _Honkai: Star Rail_, **March 7th** is often headcanoned or analyzed by fans as **MTF** (male-to-female transgender) primarily because her lore serves as a strong metaphor for the trans experience. \[[1](https://www.hoyolab.com/article/36457973)]

While there is no official confirmation from the developer, HoYoverse, that she is transgender, the community identifies several "trans-coded" elements in her story:

- **A "Rebirth" and New Name**: March 7th was discovered frozen in a block of "Six-Phased Ice" with no memory of her past. She chose her own name based on the date she was "born" into her new life, which many fans liken to a trans person choosing a new name during their transition.
- **The Loss of the Past**: Her missing past is often interpreted as a symbolic "pre-transition" life that no longer represents who she is now. In her companion quest, "Total Recall," it is suggested that her amnesia might be a choice or a form of protection, which some players relate to moving on from a past identity.
- **Self-Expression and Joy**: March 7th is known for her love of photography and cute fashion. This focus on documenting her new life and experimenting with her appearance is seen by some as an allegory for the euphoria of finally living as one's true self.
- **Magical Transition Theory**: Some fan theories suggest her "Six-Phased Ice" may have functioned as a sci-fi/fantasy artifact that physically altered her body to match her true identity during her long slumber in space. \[[1](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/March%5F7th%5F%28Honkai:%5FStar%5FRail%29), [2](https://honkai-star-rail.fandom.com/wiki/March%5F7th/Lore), [3](https://www.hoyolab.com/article/36457973), [4](https://honkai-star-rail.fandom.com/wiki/March%5F7th), [5](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KSZEEgQ0Wns\&t=92), [6](https://www.reddit.com/r/honkaisapphicrail/comments/13df9jf/does%5Fthis%5Fsub%5Fagree%5Fthat%5Fmarch%5F7th%5Fis%5Ftrans%5Fcoded/)]

Outside of gaming, it is worth noting that **March 31st** is the [International Transgender Day of Visibility](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/International%5FTransgender%5FDay%5Fof%5FVisibility), which occasionally causes confusion with the character's date-based name. \[[1](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/International%5FTransgender%5FDay%5Fof%5FVisibility), [2](https://www.instagram.com/reel/DX0Tx--PP9q/#:~:text=Yesterday%2C%20March%2031st%2C%20was%20Trans%20Day%20of,and%20time%20to%20really%20take%20time%20and)]
