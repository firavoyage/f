# 2

what does it do more than chatgpt

---

NotebookLM is built around your own information in a way that ChatGPT isn’t — at least not by default.

Here’s the difference simply:

**ChatGPT**
• Has a broad world-model trained on lots of text up to a certain point.
• Answers general questions, gives ideas, writes, explains things it has seen patterns of.
• Doesn’t automatically *know your documents* unless you paste them in.

**NotebookLM**
• Learns *your documents* — the files and notes you upload — deeply.
• Answers questions with reference to *that specific material*.
• Can connect pieces across different documents you gave it.
• Doesn’t hallucinate other facts outside what you’ve provided.

So if ChatGPT is like a smart friend who knows a lot about everything in general, NotebookLM is like a friend who has read *your stack of papers and notes carefully* and can speak with precision about them.

NotebookLM won’t replace ChatGPT for open-ended creativity or general life questions, but for *understanding and working with your own texts* it can be more focused.
