Decades later, the term naturally adapted to the internet. The meme originates from a dark and controversial reality. In a serious context, a "twisting institute" (扭转机构) is the Chinese term used to describe [**Conversion Therapy** facilities](https://zh.wikipedia.org/zh-tw/%E6%89%AD%E8%BD%89%E6%B2%BB%E7%99%82). \[[1](https://zh.wikipedia.org/zh-tw/%E6%89%AD%E8%BD%89%E6%B2%BB%E7%99%82), [2](https://www.sohu.com/a/307624847%5F502689)]
