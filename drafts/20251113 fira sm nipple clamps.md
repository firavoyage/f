- **Larger Clips (Medium/Large):** These have a much stronger spring ($F$), but their clamping surface area ($A$) is also significantly larger. This larger area can distribute the force more widely, potentially resulting in a **less focused, less sharp pinch** than a small clip, even though the total strength of the spring is greater.
