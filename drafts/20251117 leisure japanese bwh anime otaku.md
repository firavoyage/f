2.  **Assumed Ribcage Size:** To maintain the illusion of a ridiculously tiny waist like $53 \text{ cm}$, the character is often _implicitly_ assumed to also have a ribcage (Underbust) that is also extremely small, perhaps just a few centimeters larger than the waist (e.g., $60 \text{ cm}$ to $65 \text{ cm}$), even though this is biologically impossible.
3.  **Cup Size Calculation:** Since the official stats don't provide the **Underbust**, fans must estimate it. They often choose an extremely small Underbust number ($60 \text{ cm}$ or $65 \text{ cm}$) precisely because the listed **Waist** is so small. This small assumed Underbust is what mathematically drives the Bust size up to the "ridiculous" cup sizes (like H, J, or K) that the art visually suggests.

**In summary:**

- **Real World:** Waist is distinct from Underbust.
- **Anime/Doujin:** The ridiculously small **Waist (W)** _informs the fan's assumption_ that the missing **Underbust** is also tiny, allowing the **Bust (B)** to translate into an ultra-large cup size. The W-measurement _justifies_ the assumption of a small underbust.
