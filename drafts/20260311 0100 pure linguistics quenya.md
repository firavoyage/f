* **sila** – *shines* (present tense of the verb **sil-**, to shine)

* **omentielvo** – *of our meeting* (from **omentie** *meeting*, and **-lvo** is a possessive ending meaning “of us” or “our”)

This phrase is famously spoken by Galadriel in *The Fellowship of the Ring* when she greets the members of the Fellowship.

In terms of the grammar, you can see the word order is a little different from English, but still understandable once broken down.
