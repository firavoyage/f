how to say the

In English, there are two primary ways to pronounce the word **"the"**, depending on the sound of the word that follows it. \[[1](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/ZwHwV0Jn-hA), [2](https://www.britannica.com/dictionary/eb/qa/Two-Pronunciations-of-The)]

- **"Thuh" (/ðə/)**: Used before words starting with a **consonant sound**.
  - _Examples:_ The dog, the car, the house.
- **"Thee" (/ðiː/)**: Used before words starting with a **vowel sound**.
  - _Examples:_ The apple, the elephant, the ice cream. \[[1](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=C85a5uUItHc\&t=19), [2](https://www.vedantu.com/english/how-to-pronounce-the-in-english-two-pronunciations), [3](https://www.facebook.com/pronunciationwithemma/posts/how-to-pronounce-the-in-english-there-are-two-ways-to-pronounce-thelearn-the-rul/885225506976903/), [4](https://www.espressoenglish.net/how-to-pronounce-the-word-the-in-english/), [5](https://www.britannica.com/dictionary/eb/qa/Two-Pronunciations-of-The)]

Key Exceptions

The rule is based on **sound**, not spelling: \[[1](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/46oTKe5lYwI), [2](https://www.englishclub.com/pronunciation/the.php), [3](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Zy0c%5Fd9VJLk), [4](https://prepedu.com/en/blog/pronounce-the-in-english), [5](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/ZwHwV0Jn-hA)]

- **Vowel letters with consonant sounds**: Use "thuh" for words like _the university_ or _the union_ because they start with a "y" sound.
- **Consonant letters with vowel sounds**: Use "thee" for words like _the hour_ or _the MP3 player_ because the "h" or "M" creates a vowel sound at the start. \[[1](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/ZwHwV0Jn-hA), [2](https://www.vedantu.com/english/how-to-pronounce-the-in-english-two-pronunciations), [3](https://www.facebook.com/pronunciationwithemma/posts/how-to-pronounce-the-in-english-there-are-two-ways-to-pronounce-thelearn-the-rul/885225506976903/)]

Special Cases

- **Emphasis**: You can use the "thee" (/ðiː/) pronunciation to emphasize a word, even if it starts with a consonant. For example, "I met **the** (/ðiː/) President".
- **Physical Tip**: To make the "th" sound correctly, place the tip of your tongue gently between your teeth and vibrate your vocal cords while blowing air. \[[1](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=efkSdC-M%5FfU), [2](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TtpDSv2IeaM), [3](https://www.reddit.com/r/EnglishLearning/comments/uozx0x/how%5Fto%5Fsay%5Fthe/), [4](https://www.englishclub.com/pronunciation/the.php), [5](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=C85a5uUItHc\&t=19)]
