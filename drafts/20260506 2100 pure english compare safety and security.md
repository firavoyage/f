compare safety and security

The primary difference between safety and security lies in the **intent** of the threat. <mark style="margin: 0px; text-decoration-style: solid; text-decoration-color: rgb(230, 232, 240); border-bottom: 0px none rgb(230, 232, 240);"><strong>Safety</strong> focuses on protection against accidental hazards and unintended harm, such as slips, fires, or technical malfunctions. <strong>Security</strong> focuses on protection against intentional, malicious acts, such as theft, vandalism, or cyberattacks</mark>. \[[1](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/making-distinction-understanding-differences-between-safety-elmor), [2](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/ken-okelloh-82200991%5Fkey-difference-between-safety-and-security-activity-7404616417289150464-U0XS), [3](https://www.vedantu.com/english/difference-between-safety-and-security), [4](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/kevin-charagu-b41067212%5Funderstanding-the-difference-between-safety-activity-7317179586365087744-XsO2), [5](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/hse-khelfaoui-05934480%5Fsafety-vs-security-understanding-the-activity-7310945771732803584-CWQL), [6](https://www.hsetraining.org/blog/difference-between-the-safety-and-security)]

Contextual Examples

- **Building Design**: In safety terms, an emergency exit must be easy to open from the inside so people can escape quickly. In security terms, that same door should be locked from the outside to prevent unauthorized entry.
- **Software/IT**: Software safety ensures the program is reliable and won't crash or cause physical harm (like a medical device failing). Software security (cybersecurity) protects that code from being hacked or manipulated by an attacker.
- **Relationships**: Emotional safety is the feeling of being protected from immediate criticism or aggression. Security is the long-term stability and predictability of the relationship over time. \[[1](https://devops.com/software-safety-vs-security-whats-the-difference/), [2](https://elenkhos.fr/en/security-and-safety/), [3](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=F0APqi-zfwY), [4](https://www.jenniferaustinhypnotherapy.co.uk/hypnotherapyblog/security-or-safety), [5](https://www.adelastone.com/post/the-difference-between-safety-and-security-in-a-relationship), [6](https://www.tuev-nord.de/en/knowledge/explore/whats-the-difference-between-safety-and-security/), [7](https://medium.com/@nahla.benmosbah/safety-or-security-now-you-can-tell-the-difference-591a58d0f480)]

The "Security for Safety" Relationship

These two concepts are increasingly interdependent, especially in "Industry 4.0" where machines are networked. If a system's **security** is breached (e.g., a hacker gains control of a self-driving car), it directly compromises the **safety** of the passengers. Consequently, many modern frameworks now treat security as a prerequisite for safety. \[[1](https://www.perforce.com/blog/kw/software-safety-vs-security-whats-different), [2](https://www.tuev-nord.de/en/knowledge/explore/whats-the-difference-between-safety-and-security/), [3](https://medium.com/@nahla.benmosbah/safety-or-security-now-you-can-tell-the-difference-591a58d0f480)]
