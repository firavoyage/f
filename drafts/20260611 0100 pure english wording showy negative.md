The word <mark style="margin: 0px; text-decoration-style: solid; text-decoration-color: rgb(230, 232, 240); border-bottom: 0px none rgb(230, 232, 240);"><strong>"showy" can be negative, neutral, or positive</strong> depending entirely on the context in which it is used</mark>. \[[1](https://homework.study.com/explanation/what-does-showy-mean.html)]

### 🔴 When it is Negative (Connotation: Flashy or Tasteless) \[[1](https://vdict.com/showy,7,0,0.html), [2](https://ludwig.guru/s/showy)]

When applied to people, behavior, possessions, or clothing, "showy" often carries a negative connotation. According to the [Merriam-Webster Dictionary](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/showy), it implies an imposing appearance that suggests **cheapness, poor taste, or pretension**. \[[1](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/showy), [2](https://ludwig.guru/s/showy)]

- **Personality**: Describes someone who is attention-seeking or trying too hard to impress.
- **Style**: Implies something is gaudy, loud, or flashy rather than elegant.
- _Example_: _"He wore a showy gold watch that looked cheap and out of place."_ \[[1](https://www.yourdictionary.com/showy), [2](https://kids.wordsmyth.net/we/?rid=38207\&level=3), [3](https://kids.wordsmyth.net/we/?rid=38207\&level=2), [4](https://ludwig.guru/s/showy)]

### 🟢 When it is Positive or Neutral (Connotation: Vibrant or Striking) \[[1](https://kids.wordsmyth.net/we/?rid=38207\&level=5)]

When applied to nature, performances, or art, "showy" is often a positive description meaning **vivid, bright, and beautiful**. \[[1](https://homework.study.com/explanation/what-does-showy-mean.html), [2](https://vdict.com/showy,7,0,0.html)]

- **Nature**: Used by botanists and gardeners to describe large, colorful flowers or birds with striking plumage.
- **Entertainment**: Describes costumes or displays meant to be highly visual and entertaining.
- _Example_: _"The garden was filled with showy pink peonies that caught everyone's eye."_ \[[1](https://homework.study.com/explanation/what-does-showy-mean.html), [2](https://vdict.com/showy,7,0,0.html), [3](https://www.collinsdictionary.com/us/dictionary/english/showy), [4](https://www.dictionary.com/browse/gaudy), [5](https://kids.wordsmyth.net/we/?rid=38207\&level=5)]

### Quick Comparison

- **If you mean "impressive but tasteful"**: Use _striking_, _vibrant_, or _dazzling_.
- **If you mean "annoyingly prideful"**: Use _ostentatious_, _pretentious_, or _flashy_. \[[1](https://vdict.com/showy,7,0,0.html), [2](https://ludwig.guru/s/showy), [3](https://www.merriam-webster.com/word-of-the-day/ostentatious-2014-01-17), [4](https://www.vocabulary.com/dictionary/flashy)]
