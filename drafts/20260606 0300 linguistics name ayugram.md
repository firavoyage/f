- **Japanese Origin (Cultural/Aesthetic)**: In Japanese, _Ayu_ is a common feminine given name. It is also the word for the _Ayu_ (sweetfish), an iconic freshwater fish in Japan known as the "queen of clear rivers" because it only survives in pristine, unpolluted water.
