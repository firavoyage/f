### Mathematical Substance

- **The Problem:** The DGG conjecture is a foundational open problem in graph theory regarding single-source unsplittable flows. It states that any fractional flow can be rounded to an unsplittable flow without exceeding the fractional cost, given a bounded capacity violation. [link](https://eu.36kr.com/en/p/3907657849361795)
