extend or expand

<mark style="margin: 0px; text-decoration-style: solid; text-decoration-color: rgb(230, 232, 240); border-bottom: 0px none rgb(230, 232, 240);">Use <strong>extend</strong> when increasing something in a linear way, like length or time, and use <strong>expand</strong> when increasing something in volume, area, or multiple directions</mark>. Think of "extend" as making a straight line longer, and "expand" as a balloon inflating in every direction. \[[1](https://www.italki.com/en/post/question-395581), [2](https://www.italki.com/en/post/question-129144)]

When to Use "Extend"

- **Time and Deadlines**: Prolonging a period or date.
  - _Example_: "I need to **extend** my visa."
  - _Example_: "The teacher agreed to **extend** the deadline."
- **Linear Space**: Making a physical object longer.
  - _Example_: "We can **extend** the table by adding a leaf."
  - _Example_: "The city will **extend** the subway line."
- **Offerings**: Reaching out to give something.
  - _Example_: "They want to **extend** a warm welcome." \[[1](https://hinative.com/questions/5990740), [2](https://www.oxfordlearnersdictionaries.com/definition/american%5Fenglish/extend), [3](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/9%5FaW56ppYcI), [4](https://prepedu.com/en/blog/extend-vs-expand-in-english), [5](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=I9Ee8c-YrEk\&vl=en\&t=1)]

When to Use "Expand"

- **Size and Volume**: Making something physically larger or filled out.
  - _Example_: "The metals **expand** when heated."
  - _Example_: "A balloon will **expand** as you blow air into it."
- **Business and Scale**: Increasing market reach, operations, or product variety.
  - _Example_: "The company wants to **expand** into Europe."
- **Abstract Concepts**: Increasing capacity, scope, or broad knowledge.
  - _Example_: "Reading helps to **expand** your vocabulary." \[[1](https://learningenglish.voanews.com/a/extend-expand-etc-/7980779.html), [2](https://www.quora.com/What-is-the-difference-between-expand-and-extend?no%5Fredirect=1), [3](https://prepedu.com/en/blog/extend-vs-expand-in-english), [4](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=I9Ee8c-YrEk\&vl=en\&t=1), [5](https://www.instagram.com/reel/DEpc0bhJphC/)]
