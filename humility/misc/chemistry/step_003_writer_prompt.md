**3.2 Types of Chemical Bonds**

There are several types of chemical bonds, including:

* Ionic bonds: formed between atoms that transfer electrons, resulting in the formation of ions with opposite charges.
* Covalent bonds: formed between atoms that share electrons, resulting in the formation of molecules.
* Metallic bonds: formed between atoms in metals, resulting in the formation of a \"sea\" of electrons that surrounds the positively charged ions.
* Hydrogen bonds: weak bonds formed between atoms that are not directly bonded, but are attracted to each other due to the presence of hydrogen atoms.
